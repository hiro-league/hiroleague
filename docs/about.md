## **HIRO LEAGUE**
### A Personal AI Team — Private, Powerful, Always On

---

### **Core Concept**

Hiro League is a team of named, personality-driven AI agents that live on your personal desktop. Unlike cloud-based assistants, they operate entirely within your private environment — with full access to your data, devices, home, and digital life. They work together 24/7, proactively and reactively, requiring minimal effort from you.

---

### **The Agents**

Each agent has a distinct name, personality, and role — but they collaborate as a unified team. Think of them as a personal staff, not a chatbot:

- **Personal Assistant** — scheduling, reminders, tasks, file management
- **Life Coach / Therapist** — emotional support, habit tracking, mood awareness
- **Research Agent** — answers questions, monitors news, tracks your interests
- **Home & Family Agent** — manages IoT, monitors cameras, coordinates family activity
- **Social & Media Agent** — filters social media, surfaces what matters to you

---

### **What They Have Access To**

**Your Data & Devices**
- Local files, documents, photos
- Conversation history (WhatsApp, messages, email)
- Calendar, contacts, reminders
- Mobile & tablet (via companion app)
- Desktop hardware and installed apps

**Your Home Network**
- Smart home / IoT devices
- Router & connected family devices
- Home security cameras
- Other household members' devices (with permission)

**Your Digital Life**
- Social media feeds
- News and web search
- Personal interests and saved topics
- Memory of past conversations and preferences

---

### **How You Interact**

A single app — available on **mobile, web, and desktop** — gives you access to all agents through:

- **Organized channels** — each agent has its own space, like a team chat
- **3D avatar interface** — agents are represented by photorealistic Unreal Engine MetaHuman characters you can speak to
- **Natural conversation** — type, voice, or video
- **Proactive notifications** — agents reach out to *you* when something matters

---

### **What They Can Do**

| Category | Capabilities |
|---|---|
| **Awareness** | Monitor cameras, detect who's home, track family activity |
| **Memory** | Remember your preferences, past conversations, important dates |
| **Research** | Search the web, monitor news in your interest areas, summarize social media |
| **Communication** | Search your WhatsApp/email for info, draft messages, send alerts |
| **Automation** | Set prompt-based reminders, schedule tasks, control smart home |
| **Insight** | Analyze your data to give you meaningful, personalized insights |
| **Extensibility** | Create custom tools on demand, install community tools from a marketplace |

---

### **Key Differentiators**

**🔒 Private by Design** — Everything runs on *your* machine. Your data never leaves your environment.

**🧠 Proactive Intelligence** — Agents don't just answer — they anticipate, monitor, and act.

**👥 Team Dynamics** — Multiple specialized agents collaborate, not a single generalist.

**⚙️ Fully Programmable** — Instruct agents with natural language prompts. Define their behavior, goals, and triggers.

**🏪 Community Marketplace** — Extend capabilities with community-built tools and agent modules.

**🌐 Always On** — Running 24/7 on your desktop, connected to everything that matters.

---

This is essentially a **private AI operating system for your life** — one that knows you deeply, works tirelessly, and gets smarter over time.
